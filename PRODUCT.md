# CLIENTR

## Product purpose
AI-powered lead generation tool for creative agencies. Solo designers and small agencies use it to find prospects, get personalised outreach written, and track conversations — without doing cold outreach themselves.

## Users
- Solo designers (web, brand, motion) who hate prospecting but need clients.
- Founders of 2–6 person creative studios.
- Freelancers transitioning from contracting to running an agency.

They are visually literate, sceptical of generic SaaS, value craft. They will judge the product on first load: a clumsy table or a cliché purple gradient and they bounce.

## Register
Product. Design serves the work. The dashboard is a daily tool, the search page is the magic moment.

## Tone
Dark, precise, premium. The vibe of a high-end creative studio's internal tool — not a consumer app, not a corporate dashboard. Confident, quiet, expensive.

## Anti-references
- No purple gradients. No purple accents. No purple anything.
- No generic SaaS blue (#3B82F6 family).
- No glassmorphism, no fake skeuomorphism.
- No emoji-heavy interfaces.
- No cookie-cutter Stripe-clone admin.
- No light mode (dark only).
- No identical feature-card grids on the dashboard.

## Strategic principles
- The dashboard table is the product. It must feel like a premium CRM, not a side project.
- The search experience is the magic. Type intent, get real prospects + pre-written outreach back.
- Outreach copy quality matters more than UI polish — if the AI writes generic templates the product fails.
- Free tier exists to prove value (3 searches, 10 leads). Pro is £29.99/month, unlimited.
- Accent green (#00FF87) is rare. Max 5 uses per page. The interface earns its colour.
